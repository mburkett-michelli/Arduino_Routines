Can be powered with USB.  I prefer to use external power source.
Connection should be 7 to 12 VDC range for the barrel connector.

For serial connections hook in serial board.
When uploading a program make sure serial board switch is in the OFF position.
You have to power cycle after making this selection.

To use the serial port hat you must have the serial card switch in the ON position

To upload a program always use the USB cable.  When its connected it should come up
on the com port list of the Arduino IDE and identify the type of arduino board its connected
to once that has been setup in the sketch.
