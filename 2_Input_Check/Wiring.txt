PLDuino wiring:

Digital In:
Negative from 12vdc to COM
+12vDC through 120VAC Relay Common-NO Contact to D37
+12vDC through Push Button to D36

Serial3 is referenced for the USB connection
Serial is referenced for the on board serial

RS-232 9pin to RS232 on PLDuino:
Female Pin2 to TXD
Female Pin3 to RXD
Female Pin5 to GND
